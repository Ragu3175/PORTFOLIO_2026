# Portfolio Cinematic Choreography (Hybrid v3 Master Plan)
**Date: 2026-04-17**

This document tracks the frame-by-frame orchestration of the portfolio sections and the high-fidelity transition engine.

## Scene 01: The Core Arrival (Hero)
- **Visuals**: Canvas-rendered WebP frame scrubbing (240 frames).
- **Core Loop**: While stationary, the canvas slowly cycles between ±3 frames to maintain a "living" texture.
- **Scroll Logic**: Scroll progress `0 -> 1` maps linearly to frame `0 -> 240`.
- **Motion Parallax**: The `canvas` element responds to mouse X/Y with a subtle `rotateY` (-5deg to 5deg) and `scale` (1 to 1.05) via GSAP.
- **Overlay**: A radial mask `#0A0A0F` at 45% opacity fades to 80% as the scene reaches its exit.

## Scene 02: Core Identity (IdentityBreak)
- **Trigger**: `ScrollTrigger` activates at `top 80%` of viewport.
- **Transition**: **"Scanline Wipe"**
  - A 100vh black bar (solid `#0A0A0F`) with `scaleY: 0` starts at `top: 0`.
  - On scroll, it wipes down to `scaleY: 1` with `ease: "power3.in"`.
  - This hides the Hero canvas and "resets" the eye to center-screen.
- **Visuals**: Each word ("BUILDER", "SHIPPER", "PROBLEM SOLVER") begins with `clipPath: "inset(0 100% 0 0)"` and `blur(20px)`.
- **Motion**:
  1. "BUILDER" wipes right-to-left.
  2. "SHIPPER" wipes bottom-to-top.
  3. "PROBLEM SOLVER" words stagger 0.1s with a radial expansion.
  4. Lime `.dots` scale up from 0 to 1.4 then bounce back to 1.
- **Exit**: At scroll progress 95%, the entire word-set scales up by 1.2x and fades to `opacity: 0` with `y: -100` before unpinning.

## Scene 03: The Narrative (About)
- **Transition**: **"The Lime Line Strike"**
  - A neon Lime line horizontal bar cuts across the center.
  - It expands vertically to 100vh using `power4.inOut`, then retracts to reveal the About content.
- **Visuals**: Minimalist dark background. 
- **Choreography**: Sticky left-side title "ABOUT" with content cards sliding up on the right.
- **Design**: Neural network lines (Vanta.js) are DISABLED here to keep text readability at maximum.

## Scene 04: The Technical Arsenal (Skills)
- **Layout**: **3-Column Bento Assembly** (2fr 1fr 1fr configuration).
- **Assembly Logic**: 
  - On entrance, `Skills` container pins. 
  - Internal `skillChips` are globally hidden at `z: -500`, `opacity: 0`, and `rotateX: 60`.
  - On scroll progress `0% -> 40%`, chips fly forward into their respective module slots with `ease: "back.out(1.4)"`.
- **Neural Pulse**: 
  - SVG wires connecting "Core" skills (React, Node, AWS).
  - A CSS animation `stroke-dashoffset` runs a continuous "pulse" through the primary connections.
- **Interaction**: 
  - Mouse move over a card uses `GSAP QuickSetter` for a 3D Tilt effect.
  - The "Lime Dot" on each chip glows (`box-shadow`) when the cursor is within 50px.
- **Exit**: **"The Venetian Slam"**
  - 8 vertical black slats (Venetian blinds) slam shut from top and bottom simultaneously.
  - They flip open to reveal the Process section.

## Scene 05: The Blueprint (Process)
- **Visuals**: Interactive timeline of my workflow.
- **Choreography**: Each process step "powers up" with a neon lime glow as it enters the viewport center.

## Scene 06: The Showcase (Projects)
- **Transition**: **"Aperture Reveal"** — A circular iris mask expands from the center of the Process module to reveal the Projects 3D stack.
- **Visuals**: **3D Stacked Carousel**. Projects are layered cards.
- **Background**: **Vanta NET** activated here. Accent color of the background morphs to match the project (e.g., SafeDrive = Orange, CodePilot = Blue).

## Scene 07: The Validation (Credibility)
- **Transition**: **"Stage Curtain"** — Two vertical lime-bordered panels slide inwards, then open to reveal the Testimonial cards.
- **Visuals**: Standardized bento grid. Uniform stagger entrance (0.15s) with lime accents.

## Scene 08: The Connection (Contact)
- **Visuals**: Giant typography that fills the screen.
- **Exit**: As the user reaches the very bottom, the entire screen "zooms in" to a single point of light (The Core).

## Technical Dependencies
- **GSAP ScrollTrigger**: For all pinning and scrub orchestration.
- **GSAP Observer**: For fast, cinematic "Film Cuts".
- **Vanta.js (NET)**: Only active in Scenes 2 and 6 for performance.
- **Lenis**: Global smooth scrolling.
